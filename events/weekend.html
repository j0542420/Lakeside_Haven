Weekend Events: Tailored for Couples and Families

Spend your weekends making unforgettable memories with activities designed for everyone! Whether you're looking for a romantic escape or a family adventure, we've got you covered:

For Couples:

Enjoy an intimate evening around a lakeside bonfire, where you can toast marshmallows and share stories under the stars (as shown in the first picture). Don't miss our live music performances by the lake, offering the perfect setting to unwind together while watching the sunset (referencing the second picture).

For Families:

Gather the family for fun-filled activities like storytelling by the bonfire, scavenger hunts in nature, and arts and crafts workshops that kids will love. Enjoy live music and let the kids dance freely as the serene lakeside ambiance keeps everyone entertained.
